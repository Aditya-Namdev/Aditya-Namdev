## Project Overview
This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio, created as part of the Student Digital Ambassador assignment. It documents my work across five modules, covering digital awareness, professional online presence, collaboration tools, email etiquette, and cybercrime prevention.

## Module Summaries
* **Task 1 (Infographic):** A visual guide on digital literacy awareness.
* **Task 2 (Digital Portfolio):** Setup of professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle.
* **Task 3 (Platforms):** Coding practice on HackerRank and a collaborative Google Form quiz.
* **Task 4 (Email Etiquette):** Drafts of professional emails and a social media conduct checklist.
* **Task 5 (Cybercrime):** A case study on UPI fraud and a prevention checklist.
